Background

I'm a product designer who builds at the intersection of code, canvas, and AI. I've spent my career on platform problems — identity systems, governance workflows, and trust surfaces that need to work at scale.


I don't just design screens. I design systems that ship — and I use AI to compress the distance between insight and artifact without losing intention.

  • Why governance is a design problem, not just an engineering one
  • Building multi-agent design workflows that actually ship
  • The future of design tools is code + canvas — not one or the other
  • Executable constraints over design guidelines
